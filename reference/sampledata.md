# GDC TCGA Lung Adenocarcinoma (LUAD) - Metadata

Samples information of the subset of TCGA-LUAD RNA-seq gene-level counts
generated by STAR and distributed via UCSC Xena. Data originate from
GDC, only selected samples are included. Sample barcodes were compacted
and written with dots instead of dashes (e.g., "TCGA-38-4627-11A" →
"TCGA.38.4627.11A").

## Usage

``` r
sampledata
```

## Format

### `sampledata`

A data frame with 32 rows and 12 columns:

- patient_id:

  TCGA sample ID written with dots instead of dashes, e.g.,
  `TCGA.38.4627.11A`.

- sample_type:

  Sample category, e.g., `"tumor"` or `"normal"`.

- age:

  Age (years) at diagnosis/collection.

- race_demographic:

  Self-reported race/ethnicity (e.g., `"white"`).

- sex:

  Biological sex (`"male"` / `"female"`).

- status:

  Vital status at last follow-up (`"Alive"` / `"Dead"`).

- pathologic_stage:

  Overall AJCC pathologic stage, e.g., `"Stage IA"`, `"Stage IIIA"`.

- pathologic_t:

  Primary tumor (T) category, e.g., `"T1b"`, `"T2"`.

- smoking_status:

  Smoking history from TCGA clinical (free text), e.g.,
  `"Lifelong Non-smoker"`.

- agents:

  Therapeutic agents administered (free text; may be empty).

- treatment_response:

  Clinical response to therapy (free text; may be empty).

- treatment_type:

  Type of therapy (free text; may be empty).
